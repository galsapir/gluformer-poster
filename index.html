The Headline Result

65.8% of new-onset diabetes in GluFormer's top risk quartile vs. 7.3% in bottom quartile
69.2% of cardiovascular deaths in the top quartile 0% in bottom quartile

Two weeks of glucose monitoring encodes a decade of health trajectory ‚Äî and standard clinical metrics miss most of it.

GluFormer Architecture

  1. Tokenize ‚Äî Glucose values become discrete tokens, like words in language
  2. Pre-train ‚Äî Transformer learns glucose dynamics via next-token prediction
  3. Extract ‚Äî Internal representations encode rich metabolic phenotypes
  4. Apply ‚Äî Embeddings transfer to diverse clinical prediction tasks

Why It Matters

Diabetes affects 500M+ people worldwide (projected 1.3B by 2050). Over-the-counter CGM devices are now FDA-approved for non-diabetic adults ‚Äî creating a massive new data source for early detection.

  • 65.8% of future diabetes captured in one risk quartile ‚Äî from just 14 days of glucose data
  • AUC 0.75 vs. 0.66 for standard metrics ‚Äî across baseline, 2-year, and 4-year horizons
  • 19 cohorts, 5 countries ‚Äî generalizes across devices, ethnicities, and disease states
  • Precision nutrition ‚Äî dietary tokens enable personalized glucose response predictions
