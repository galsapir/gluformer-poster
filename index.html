The Headline Result

65.8% of new-onset diabetes in GluFormer's top risk quartile vs. 7.3% in bottom quartile
69.2% of cardiovascular deaths in the top quartile 0% in bottom quartile

Two weeks of glucose monitoring encodes a decade of health trajectory — and standard clinical metrics miss most of it.

GluFormer Architecture

How It Works

  1. Tokenize — Glucose values discretized into 460 tokens, like words in language
  2. Pre-train — 16-layer transformer learns next-token prediction on >10M measurements
  3. Extract — Internal representations encode rich metabolic phenotypes
  4. Apply — Embeddings transfer to diverse clinical prediction tasks

Key Takeaways

  • Foundation model for CGM — Self-supervised learning on 10M+ glucose measurements
  • 19 cohorts, 5 countries, 8 devices — Universal generalization
  • Outperforms HbA1c — Superior long-term risk prediction
  • Actionable — Identifies at-risk individuals years before deterioration
  • Multi-modal — Integrates dietary data for precision nutrition
